Bookings Laboratory

Delete a booking

 

When you press My bookings button the application show your bookings

 

 If you don´t have any booking you will see the next screen

 

 If you have several bookings select the booking that you want to delete and press Delete button.

You will receive a final confirmation in your email address

 

 

The application doesn´t allow to delete bookings which start date is equal to today date, you will receive a warning.

 

 

If you wish to make another booking press Book button
